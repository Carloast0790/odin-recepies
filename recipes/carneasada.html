Carne Asada

This delicious dish can be seing in every mexican barbecue. You can find every ingredient in your local market.

Ingredients

  • Beef meat
  • Tortillas
  • Onion and Parsil
  • Avocado
  • A grill

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. In the mean time, cut and mix the parsil and onion in small pieces
  3. Heat the tortilla
  4. Add the meat to plate and eat it with the tortillas and the sliced vegetables
