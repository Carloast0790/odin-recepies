Tacos al pastor

This delicious dish is typical in Mexico. You can find every ingredient in your local market.

Ingredients

  • Al pastor meat
  • Tortillas
  • Onion and Parsil
  • Pinaple

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Add chopped pieces of pinaple and let cook for five minutes
  3. In the mean time, cut and mix the parsil and onion in small pieces
  4. Heat the tortilla
  5. Add the meat in the tortilla and on top the sliced vegetables
