Enchiladas

This delicious dish is typical in Mexico. You may find it hard to find every ingredient in your local market. Don't get discouraged, it is worth the trouble!

Ingredients

  • Chile ancho and Morita
  • Tortillas
  • Onion and Parsil
  • Cheese, we recomend fresh cheese

Steps

  1. On a hot pan add a spoon of oil and let the chiles cook for a while
  2. Add the tomatoes, onion, parsil and garlic
  3. Once cooked, add to the blender and mix everithing to make the sauce
  4. In the mean time, cut and mix the parsil and onion in small pieces
  5. Plunge the tortillas in the sauce and add them to the pan
  6. Let it cook for a while and turn around
  7. Remove from the pan and serve it
  8. Add on top the sliced vegetables
